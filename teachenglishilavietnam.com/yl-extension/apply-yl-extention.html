Apply now to YL Extention course

Following Cambridge English entry regulations, our application process is designed to help us select candidates who:

  • are 18 or over
  • have the standard of education required for entering higher education.
  • have the potential to become effective teachers.
  • demonstrate that they are likely to complete the course successfully.
  • are competent users of the language and who have an awareness of language that enables them to manage the demands of the course.

Please complete the applications form in below:

The YL Extension is the most internationally recognized teaching certificate in the world. ILA run 10 YL Extension courses a year and upon completion many successful candidates go on to work with us here in Vietnam.

Please leave your profile and complete the YL Extension application pack. A member of Teacher Training Department will be in touch shortly to provide further information.
