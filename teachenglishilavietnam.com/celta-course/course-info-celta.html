CELTA course Information

During the course, you will have 120 hours of contact in total. The course usually runs from Monday to Friday from 8.45am - 5.30pm.

Outside of these contact hours, you can expect an additional workload of 3-4 hours each night and that at least 1 of your weekend days will be spent writing assignments and lesson plans. Because of this intensity, the course is a full-time commitment for each candidate, so working whilst doing the course is not recommended.

Course Payment

You can pay for courses in cash or by credit card at any of our training centres.

From overseas you can pay by bank transfer or online using a credit card.

Terms and Conditions

Refunds

Once paid, deposits are non-refundable. If you would like more information about refunds please contact us. In the unlikely event that the course does not run, we will notify you as soon as possible and offer you either; a place on a subsequent course or a full refund on your payment. Please be aware that there is a risk of failing the CELTA course and in such cases no money will be returned.

Withdrawals

People withdraw from the course for various reasons and this will be discussed with you at the point of interview. Please note that candidates who withdraw from a course after it has started will not be refunded, nor will they be transferred to a future course (except in special circumstances*).

Special Circumstances

Applicants are required to declare any health problems that they feel may affect their progress during the application process. Failure to do so may negate the opportunity for any necessary extra support. Medical reports may be requested by the centre.

Medical problems that occur during the course will be examined on a case-by-case basis.

If a candidate suffers a serious medical issue during the course and wishes to withdraw for medical reasons, a medical note must be provided to the centre. In this case, the candidate may be offered a deferral of fees to a future course.

Course Reimbursements

A settling-in allowance of 16,000,000VND (approx. $750) gross is available to our CELTA graduates who sign a full-time teaching contract with ILA within 6 months of completing their course. This is paid after the first completed month of the teaching contract.
