Teach English at ILA Vietnam

ILA is Vietnam's leading English language training organisation. We have teaching centres in Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, Da Nang, Vung Tau and Binh Duong and we are growing steadily. We started in 2006 and are now one of Vietnam’s largest English training centers with currently over 400 teachers. We offer excellent career development and training opportunities as well as a world class teaching environment.


We are also one of Asia's largest training centres for Cambridge English Teaching Qualifications. We have successfully trained over 1,200 teachers in CELTA certificate courses, DELTA certificate courses and YL Extension courses.
