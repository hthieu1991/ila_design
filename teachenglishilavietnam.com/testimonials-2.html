Some words from our happy english teacher

P. GannonTeacher

Lots of support for teachers, particularly new teachers. Systematic professional development through workshops, observation feedback and setting professional goals. Academic Managers all have years of experience and a DELTA. Their number one job is to support and develop teachers.

Lots of opportunities for promotion for teachers. ILA is opening many new centres which all will need Senior Teachers, Academic Coordinators and Academic Managers. Teachers can also move into HR recruitment and the teaching training department.
