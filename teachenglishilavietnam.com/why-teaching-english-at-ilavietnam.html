Why teaching english at ILA Vietnam ?

ILA Vietnam is a foreign-owned education and training company that offers a broad range of educational programs and services. In 2013 we educated more than 35,000 students at ILA centres in Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, Da Nang and Vung Tau becoming a clear leader in the Vietnamese market



Our training center and facilities

Our Training Centre

Our teacher training courses take place in a thriving language school where you will have access to the various resources and facilities of the institution, and to our experienced teachers and trainers. We have dedicated classrooms, preparation rooms and computers for all of our trainees. The school itself is right in the bustling middle of HCMC surrounded by restaurants, cafes and hotels.

Ho Chi Minh - The Cosmopolitan Hub of Vietnam

Ho Chi Minh City is Vietnam's largest city and is a vibrant and bustling place teeming with opportunities. It is full of life and excitement, from colourful street markets to world-class restaurants; from renovated colonial buildings to ancient pagodas hidden amongst the tangled alleyways. While every corner provides opportunities for new experiences, the comforts of home can be easily found.

Ho Chi Minh City is the main location for ILA schools, with centres spread around the city in different districts. From the biggest to the smallest, ILA schools are all professional centres with an enthusiastic group of teachers and staff dedicated to bringing quality English language teaching to students.
